Ingredients: 2 Cans of Cream of Mushroom soup, 2 large cans of Tuna Fish (bumbletuna preferred), clam shell noodles, 1 bag of shredded cheddar cheese
Step 1:  Cook the noodles until they are al dente
Step 2:  Drain the tuna fish
Step 3:  Combine cream of mushroom soup, noodles, and Tuna fish in a baking dish.  
Step 4:  Bake in oven at 350 Degress for 30 minutes.  
Step 5:  Take the tuna mac out of the oven and sprinkle entire chedar cheese bag on top of tuna mac
Step 6:  Place baking dish back in oven for 5 minutes
Step 7:  Allow dish to cool slightly and serve
